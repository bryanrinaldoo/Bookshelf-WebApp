FAQ

What is Bookshelf?
Bookshelf is a web application that helps you to manage your book. Bookshelf was made to fulfill an assignment at Dicoding. You can add your new book to Bookshelf easily. Bookshelf divided into two shelves, 'Reading' and 'Done'. The 'Reading' is the shelf that contains books that you are currently reading. As for 'Done' is the shelf that contains books that you already completed
What feature that this web application provide?
1. You can add your new book at the top of the page. You can add to the 'Reading' shelf or the 'Done' shelf.
2. You can move your book from 'Reading' shelf to 'Done' shelf and vice versa.
3. You can edit your book by clicking on the title of the book.
